AV2D

Introducing AV2D: Save Isabella! Join Gort, the courageous protagonist, on a mission to rescue Isabella from the clutches of the notorious villain, Spike. Your goal is to complete the game by embarking on an exciting journey through 10 thrilling levels.

Here's a pro tip: You can give yourself a boost by utilizing corners strategically. This technique will help you overcome challenging situations and progress further in the game.

If you find yourself in need of assistance to conquer a level, you can check out the demo walkthrough on YouTube. Watch the walkthrough to gain valuable insights and overcome any hurdles that come your way.

AV2D was developed using the powerful Unity Engine and incorporates free-to-use assets obtained from itch.io, ensuring a visually appealing and immersive gaming experience. Keep in mind that the version available is the demo, with the full release coming soon™.

Save Isabella and become the hero she needs!
